Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Horizon.AI respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

  • Name (if provided)
  • Email address
  • Authentication information via Supabase
  • Chat history (if chat saving is enabled)
  • Device and browser information
  • Anonymous analytics used to improve the service

Authentication

Authentication is securely handled through Supabase Authentication. Passwords are never stored directly by Horizon.AI.

AI Requests

Messages you send may be forwarded to selected AI providers (such as OpenRouter, Groq, Google Gemini, or others you choose) in order to generate responses.

Cookies

Horizon.AI may use essential cookies or browser storage to keep you signed in and remember preferences such as theme, settings, and selected AI models.

Data Storage

User information may be stored securely using Supabase services. Appropriate security measures are used to protect your information.

Your Rights

  • Access your information
  • Request correction
  • Delete your account
  • Request deletion of stored data where applicable

Third-party Services

Horizon.AI integrates with third-party services including Supabase, Vercel, Google OAuth, GitHub OAuth, and AI providers. Those services are governed by their own privacy policies.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the Horizon.AI team through the official GitHub repository.
